# Powerbi-Dashboard
Developed an interactive sales dashboard to analyze Nykaa's sales performance, customer purchasing trends, and product category insights for the year 2017-2022.
Here is a detailed brekdown of insights.


### 1. Customer Demographics and behaviour
* Total Customers: 300 | Avg Age: 35.94 | Total Qty: 6K | Avg Spend: 17.18K
* Membership Tiers: Regular (35.67%), Platinum (32.67%), Gold (31.67%)
* Insight: Revenue spans ages 18–55, with an unexpected buying surge around ages 46–47.

### 2. Sales insights
* Total Sales: 5M | Total Profit: 1.15M | AOV: 2.58K.
* Category Share: Skincare (50.38%), Makeup (39.17%), Haircare (10.45%).
* Top Product: Lakme Lipstick leads volume sales.

### 3. Returns & Quality Insights 
* Total Returns: 200 | Return Rate: 10% | Returned Sales: 525K.
* Avg Days to Return: 4.03 days.
* Main Return Drivers: Wrong Item Shipped (35%) and Damaged Products (34.5%).
* Vulnerability: Skincare suffers the highest operational return volume.
